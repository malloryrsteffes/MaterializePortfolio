About

I grew up in Florida, but have lived in a few cool places in my life - NYC, San Francisco, and Dallas are my favorites. I went to the University of Florida for my undergraduate theatre degree, worked an internship in California, then got a great job with a fantastic company in NYC, working in private event planning.

Even though I loved my job, I was feeling pretty unfulfilled. While in NYC, I tried out a very basic intro coding workshop, and actually absolutely hated it. None of it came naturally to me, and I felt so discouraged! It took some self-reflection (and some sense from friends), but I realized I'd actually have to buckle down and commit 100% if I wanted to do this. I found UCF's coding bootcamp, and moved as soon as I could!

Being momentarily unemployed is new territory for me, but luckily I have very little free time outside of class and homework. In my now nonexistent spare time, I'm usually playing video games, lifting weights, or indulging an unhealthy online shopping habit. I can't wait to get coding so that one day I can (hopefully) afford it!
